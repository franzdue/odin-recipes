Pizza

Description

Our homemade pizza is the best in town, as all ingredients are fresh and sourced regionally from our own farms in the countryside.
Not convinced? Come try our pizza yourself.

Ingredients

  • 125ml lukewarm water
  • 7g white-sugar
  • 3.5g active dry-yeast
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 10g native virgin olive oil
  • 230g bread flour
  • 120g freshly-grated mozarella cheese
  • tasty tomatoe sauce

Steps

  1. Dissolve sugar in water
  2. Add active dry yeast and let it for a couple of minutes
  3. Add flour, olive oil and salt, and combine it into a smooth dough
  4. Cover it with a kitchen towel and let it rest for 1 hour in a warm and dark place
  5. Roll out dough and add remaining ingredients
  6. Bake for 10-15 min in a pre-heated oven at 200Â°C
